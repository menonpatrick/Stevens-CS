My Education

This is a brief description about my Education. From where it all began. I got my bachelors degree from Andhra University, India.

Andhra University

Andhra University is a very prestigious University with more than 150 years of history to boast about. Its is a pioneer in many aspects when it comes to education, introducing various new courses and educating millions of people in its avst 150+ years of span.

In the your year span of my Bachelors degree, I attended numerous classes related to various courses, selecting a single favourite class is a difficult task but if i narrowdown to one class then that would be the SLC(Switching Logic Circuits) class. Undoubtedly the most fun packed learning sessions I ever experienced, working on bread boards, making connections using various logic gates and validating the output for the logic designed. Its good fun and great learning.
